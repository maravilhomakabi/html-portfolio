The Best Movies According to Makabi

My top 3 movies of all-time

Titanic (1997)

Titanic (1997) is an epic romance disaster film folloing Rose DeWitt Bukater, a high-society woman, and Jack Dawson, a poor artist, who fall in love aboard the ill-fated 1912 maiden voyage of the RMS Titanic.
Their romance challenges class constraints while the ship speeds toward a fatal iceberg collision, culminating in a survival story as the vessel sinks.

Apocalypto (2006)

Apocalypto (2006), directed by Mel Gibson, is a visceral survival action film set during the decline of the Mayan civilization.
It follows Jaguar Paw, a peaceful forest hunter who, after hiding his pregnant wife and son, is captured by warriors for human sacrifice, embarking on a desperate, high-stakes journey to escape and return to his family.

Blood Diamond (2006)

Blood Diamond (2006) is an action-thriller set during the 1990s Sierra Leone Civil War, focusing on the illicit trade of "conflict diamonds" used to finance armed combat.
The story follows a smuggler, a fisherman, and a journalist searching for a rare pink diamond amid extreme violence, child soldier conscription, and corporate greed.
